Proof of Reasoning

PoR stands for Proof of Reasoning. It's not marketing fluff. It's the fundamental mechanism that separates tokens with substance from tokens with just a Twitter account. Here's what it actually means: Every significant action CROW takes is backed by transparent, defensible reasoning. Not 'trust us, we're smart.' But 'here's exactly why we did this, and you can verify the logic yourself.'
Meme coins rely on belief. PoR-backed projects rely on reasoning. When belief fades, meme coins die. When reasoning improves, PoR projects get stronger.
